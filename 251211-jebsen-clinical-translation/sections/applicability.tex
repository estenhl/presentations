\begin{frame}{The applicability of research models}
    Most models trained for research are proof-of-concepts relying on simplified prediction problems and sanitized data.
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Training models for more appropriate use-cases requires clinical insights and access to clinical data.
        \item \textbf{More collaboration between technologists and clinical experts are crucial.}
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
