\begin{frame}{The black-box problem of artificial intelligence}
    Although artificial neural networks very often result in accurate predictions, their opaque nature makes it hard to understand their underlying decision processes.
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Shortcutting the problem by relying on superficial cues rather than genuine understanding can give rise to "Clever Hans"-predictors.
        \item Explainable artificial intelligence provides methodology for probing how these models work, building trust and potentially enabling clinical use.
    \end{itemize}
\end{frame}
